Leonardo Da Vinci

One of the greatest painters in the history of art.

1400

  • 1452 - Leonardo is born in Vinci, Italy.
  • 1466 - Starts apprenticeship with Andrea Veroccio.
  • 1478 - Qualifies as an independent master.
  • 1481 - The Adoration of the Magi.
  • 1484 - Begins work for the Sforza clan in Milan.
  • 1486 - Virgin of the Rocks.
  • 1490 - The Vitrubian Man.
  • 1499 - Flees to Florencia.

1500

  • 1502 - Begins work as military architect for Borgia family.
  • 1504 - La Gioconda (Monalisa).
  • 1516 - Moves to France, entering king Francis service.
  • 1519 - Dies at Ambroise, Kingdom of France.


You can learn much more about this amazing artist in his Wikipedia entry.
